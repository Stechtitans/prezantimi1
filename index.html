Our team

We are TECH TITANS, a group of 10 members from 'Stavri Themeli' who came together to create a project using our knowledge and learning new things at the same time. As mythology is part of our cultural heritage, we thought of making our country's mythology more widely known in entertaining ways.

Martina Nikolla, Zoi Qose, Athina Qirici, Darli Karamuço, Klei Mulla, Kristel Mindili, Ashli Sedo, Daria Mitre, Andrea Dhamollari

OUR GOAL

To make our mythology known from Albanians and foreigners, including individuals of all ages and children with different abilities, through the most entertaining ways such as games, quizzes, and through artificial intelligence.


OUR IDEA

To create a website that offers various information about mythology, games, quizzes, and a virtual assistant to help users navigate the site and answer any questions they may have.

ALBANIAN MYTHOLOGY

Albanian mythology is a folkloric genre composed of stories that play a fundamental role in society, such as myths, legends, and fairy tales. A myth is a fantastic narrative that explains the birth of natural elements. The purpose of a myth is to explain the origin of the world and of humans. The protagonists of myths can be gods, people with extraordinary powers, heroes, monsters, and other fantastic creatures. The time when the events occurred is not specified. Illyrian mythology and its successor, Albanian mythology, have influenced all people and the present generations by providing messages and entertainment to children. Illyro-Albanian mythology has also influenced other mythologies.
